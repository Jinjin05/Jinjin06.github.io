How to Cook SWEET AND SOUR PORK

In Chinese tradition, sauce is made by mixing sugar or honey with vinegar soy sauce and spices. In East Asia, there were some dishes varied from Sweet and Sour Pork. Like Guo Bao Rou, Squirrel-shaped Mandarin fish, Sweer and sour Yellow River carp, and a sweet and sour meat dish known as tangsuyuk.
